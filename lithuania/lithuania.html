A Sample of Lithuania

Last Update: September 2023

Beautiful seashore, basketball, aviation history: Lituanica (1933), ANBO IV (1934), Jurgis Kairys (1999).

Schools have free, modern courts available in the evenings and on weekends.

Linguistic composition in Vilnius:

  • 1897: 40% Yiddish, 31% Polish, 20% Russian, 4.2% Belarusian, 2.1% Lithuanian…

  • 1989: 51% Lithuanian, 20% Russian, 19% Polish…

  • 2021: 67% Lithuanian, 15% Polish, 10% Russian…

  • 2024? A sizable immigration occurred after February 24, 2022.

Vilnius hosted the 1984 World Chess Candidates Finals match between Garry Kasparov and Vasily Smyslov.

Also one of only three games played by the Atlanta Hawks against the USSR, 1988.

Software. Vinted, Hostinger, Kilo.Health, Teltonika, NordVPN, Nordcurrent Group, TutoToons, No Brakes Games… Foreign offices: Numerous banks, Dassault Systèmes, IBM, Canonical, Unity, Wix, Nasdaq, Kayak, Wargaming, Nokia… with all the ups and downs of early capitalism.
