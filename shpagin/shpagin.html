Александр Шпагин (2023)

This is one deep discussion about Tarkovskij’s Mirror (1975). It is also about how religion and metaphysics creeps into a completely atheistic society, inevitably, the way people would reinvent rolling dice or card games on an uninhabited island without knowing their prior existence.

Also, what makes the 1960s so special?

I will sum up here A. Shpagin’s ideas eventually, but better learn Russian and enjoy these incredible discussions. There is an entire world there: Антон Долин, Зинаида Пронченко, Любовь Аркус, Николай Солодников, Клим Жуков, Александр Гордон…

We have quite a few interesting films, and quite a few movie buffs who can write, sometimes even decipher stuff, unlike, say, in more elitarian corners of math, music, physics, programming… Raise your hand who has reached Alexander Grothendieck.

So we get mxn studies, with some genuine interest and honesty, sometimes depth. Can you expect a better community?

A. Shpagin was there in 1970s, collecting, reflecting, catching some genuine context. He leads us to The Stars of the Day (1968)…
