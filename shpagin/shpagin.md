# [Александр Шпагин (2023)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=96Aa89AVo7s&t=2s)

There is hardly a film in the entire history of cinema that has been analyzed more than Tarkovskij's Mirror (1975). Александр Шпагин (Alexander Shpagin) reveals a lot of interesting details which I have not seen elsewhere.

There are some incredible people around us with deeper vision, who aim to understand the world. They seldom publish much and nobody knows them. Some are visible. In the Russian-speaking space, I vaguely follow Lyubov Arkus, Anton Dolin, Zinaida Pronchenko, Klim Zhukov. Most of them are doomed to become superficial productive essay writers at times as they have to make a living, go for quantity rather than quality. However, each of them has a lot to say if an interviewer hits the right nerve, which does happen sometimes, when it becomes personal.

This interview, almost a monologue, is personal.
