Павел Загребельный

Павел Загребельный (Pavel Zagrebelnyy) is the main creator of Spintires: The Original Game (2014-2020). This is an amazing game about off-road cargo delivery missions with a hypnotizing level of realism regarding mud and vehicle physics.

Unbalanced rewards between Pavel Zagrebelnyy, Saber, and Oovee has led them to the mess of three subsequent games which are entirely missing the most interesting question to answer: Will a particular setup will suffice to survive, or not?

There should be at least several types of mud: (i) the denser swamps which are better handled with tracked vehicles, and (ii) those which can be conquered by the vehicles with very large diameter (>1600mm), low pressure tires.

I would want to see DT-30, Trom-8, and Trom-20, with a proper Sherp/Bigbo or Sherp Ark 10x10 which can get through wilderness for real. Syberia with Putorana Plateau, or even Kamchatka and the North, ultimately.

Imagine the best of Spintires and BeamNg.drive united into a single game.

More physics, geometry, details. A deeper forest. We do not know how deep a forest can be. Away from fake urban spaces and endless truck catalogs of the same thing in a different skin. Away from project management options and game menus. The narrowest path is always the holiest.
